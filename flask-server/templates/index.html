About Me

Welcome!  I’m a professional software engineer that has spent most of my career developing real-time embedded software.  Most of my career has been spent developing in C, but I have experience with Java and python.

Besides developing software, I enjoy cooking, researching stocks (my favorite site is The Motley Fool), watching football and baseball, and spending time with my family.  I live in the San Francisco Bay Area with my wife and our two children.
